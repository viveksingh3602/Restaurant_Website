Avocado Tomato Bruschetta

The Avocado Tomato Bruschetta is a delightful fusion of creamy avocado, juicy tomatoes, and fresh coriander (dhania) leaves, served atop a crispy slice of artisan bread.

Honey Almond Banana Oat Bowl

This dish combines hearty oats with slices of ripe banana and crunchy almonds, all lightly drizzled with golden honey for a natural sweetness.

Price: ₹ 250

Green Power Salad

The Green Power Salad is a vibrant and nutritious dish that combines the creamy richness of ripe avocado with the crisp freshness of broccoli and capsicum.

Zesty Corn Bhel

The Spicy Corn & Peanut Chaat is a zesty and flavorful snack that brings together the sweetness of corn, the tanginess of tomatoes, and the burst of blackberries, all complemented by crunchy peanuts and crispy puffed rice.
